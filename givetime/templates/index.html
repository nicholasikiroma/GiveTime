Join a Community of Changemakers

Do you want to make a positive impact in your community, but don't know where to start? GiveTime is here to help. Our web-based platform connects volunteers with non-profit organizations, making it easy to find volunteering opportunities that match your interests, skills, and availability.

With GiveTime, you can search for opportunities based on location, skills required, and cause area, and even create a profile to keep track of your impact. Join our community of volunteers and non-profit organizations today and start making a difference.

Ideal for Volunteers and Non-profit Organizations.

GiveTime offers a range of features designed to help volunteers find meaningful opportunities and non-profit organizations efficiently manage their volunteer programs.

Customizable Search Filters

GiveTime allows volunteers to search for volunteer opportunities based on their interests, skills, and location making it easier for them to find opportunities that match their preferences.

Detailed Opportunity Descriptions

Each volunteer opportunity listed on GiveTime includes a detailed description, including information about the organization, the tasks involved, the time commitment, and any other relevant details.

Volunteer Application Management

GiveTime allows volunteers to apply for volunteer opportunities directly through the platform, and non-profit organizations can review applications and follow up with further details, making it easier for both parties to manage the volunteer recruitment process.

Non-profit dashboard

Non-profit organizations have access to a dashboard that allows them to manage their volunteer opportunities, view and accept volunteer applications, and track volunteer hours and impact, providing them with the tools they need to effectively manage their volunteer programs.
