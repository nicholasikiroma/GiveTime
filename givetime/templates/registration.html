Join GiveTime and Make a Difference!

Thank you for your interest in GiveTime, the web-based platform that connects volunteers with non-profit organizations. We're excited to have you on board and help you find meaningful volunteering opportunities that match your skills, interests, and availability.

To get started, please select one of the options below to register as a nonprofit or a volunteer. If you represent a non-profit organization, you'll be able to create a profile and advertise your volunteer opportunities, manage your volunteers, and track your impact. If you're a volunteer, you'll be able to create a profile and search for volunteer opportunities that match your preferences, apply for opportunities, and track your volunteer history and progress.

Joining GiveTime is free, easy, and only takes a few minutes. Whether you're looking to give back to your community, gain new skills, or meet like-minded people, GiveTime is the perfect platform for you. So don't hesitate, click on the button below to get started and make a difference today!
